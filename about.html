About Us

Turning food waste into opportunity for everyone

Our Story

Every day, thousands of meals are thrown away while people struggle to afford food. Food Rescue Solutions was created to bridge that gap.

We partner with local restaurants to collect perfectly good surplus food and offer it at a much lower price. This helps reduce waste, supports businesses, and makes meals accessible to more people.

What We Stand For

🌱 Sustainability

We reduce food waste and help protect the environment.

💰 Affordability

We make quality meals accessible at lower prices.

🤝 Community

We connect people, restaurants, and neighborhoods.

How It Works

1. Restaurants List Food

Local restaurants upload extra meals to our platform.

2. You Order

Customers browse and purchase meals at discounted prices.

3. Pick Up & Enjoy

Pick up your food and enjoy a great meal while reducing waste.

Meet the Founder

Food Rescue Solutions was founded with a simple idea: food should never go to waste when people need it.

By combining technology with community action, we created a platform that benefits restaurants, customers, and the environment.
