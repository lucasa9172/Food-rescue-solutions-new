Frequently Asked Questions

All meals listed on our platform come from licensed local restaurants that follow strict food safety and handling guidelines. Food is stored at proper temperatures and must be listed within a safe time window before it expires. We only partner with restaurants that meet health standards, and meals are intended to be picked up and consumed the same day to ensure freshness and quality.
Restaurants often have extra food at the end of the day that would otherwise go to waste. Instead of throwing it away, they list these meals on our platform at discounted prices. This allows businesses to recover some revenue while giving customers access to high-quality meals at a lower cost. It’s a win-win situation that reduces waste and saves money.
After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation with the restaurant’s pickup location and time window. Most orders are ready within 30 to 60 minutes. Simply show your confirmation at the restaurant, and your meal will be prepared for quick pickup. This process keeps things fast and efficient for both customers and businesses.
Some meals are pre-packaged “surprise meals,” while others clearly list what is included. This depends on the restaurant and how they prepare their surplus food. Even when items vary slightly, restaurants aim to provide balanced, high-quality meals that reflect their regular menu standards.
Once your order is placed, it is sent directly to the restaurant. You will receive a confirmation and pickup instructions. When you arrive, simply show your order confirmation, collect your meal, and enjoy. If there are any issues, our support team is available to assist.
